Virat Kohli

The Cricket Legend

Virat Kohli, the Indian cricket sensation, is known for his aggressive batting style and remarkable consistency. He is one of the modern-day greats of cricket and has achieved numerous milestones throughout his career.

Key Achievements

  • Fastest century by an Indian in One Day Internationals (ODIs).
  • Fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in ODI cricket.
  • Holds the record for most centuries in run-chases in ODI cricket.
  • Named ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018.
  • Recipient of the prestigious Padma Shri award in 2017.

Virat Kohli's passion for the game and his commitment to excellence have inspired millions of cricket fans worldwide. He continues to be a role model for aspiring cricketers and is an integral part of Indian cricket's success story.
